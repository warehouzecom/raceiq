## [0.1.0] — 2026-03-31

### Added
- Race Day Dashboard — track sidebar, race list with ML prediction previews, right panel
- Race Card Detail — full runners table with ML win probability bars, expert picks, AI narrative
- Horse Profile — form history, going stats, trainer/jockey details
- ML vs Expert Comparison — side-by-side probability bars, divergence callouts, stake ratings
- ELO Leaderboard — ML model vs human experts ranked by win rate and ROI
- Fantasy Racing — stable management, weekly points, league standings
- Subscription Upgrade page — Free / Pro / Elite / Expert tier comparison
- Responsive design — mobile card views, bottom navigation, track switcher pill bar
- Mock data for MTCJC Champ de Mars (5 races, Meeting 8) and Racing & Sports Flemington/Rosehill
- Dark mode design system — brand-green probability bars, purple Elite features, amber divergence signals
